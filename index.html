OC Beach — Privacy Policy

Last updated: [5/2/26]

This Privacy Policy describes how OC Beach ("the App," "we," "us," "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the OC Beach mobile application. OC Beach is operated by [Luca Piceno] as a sole proprietor based in the State of California, United States.

By using OC Beach, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

Account information:

  • Email address (used to create and access your account)
  • Password (stored securely, never visible to us in plain text)
  • Username you choose to display publicly

Profile information you provide:

  • Profile picture (optional)
  • Home beach selection (optional)

Content you create:

  • Photos you upload as posts
  • Captions you write
  • Comments you post on other users' content
  • Direct messages and group chat messages you send
  • Likes you give on posts
  • Beach conditions snapshots attached to your posts (a record of weather and ocean conditions at the time you posted)

Information about your use of the App:

  • Reports you file about other users' content
  • Users you have blocked
  • Posts and conversations you have viewed (only as needed to provide features like unread message counts)

We do not collect:

  • Your precise location (we do not request location permission)
  • Contacts from your device
  • Health, financial, or other sensitive data outside the categories above

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide the App's core functionality (posting, messaging, profile)
  • Authenticate your account and keep it secure
  • Display content to other users (your posts and profile are publicly visible to other signed-in users by design)
  • Process reports and enforce community safety
  • Communicate with you about account or service issues
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. How We Store Your Data

Your data is stored using Supabase (https://supabase.com), a third-party cloud database and authentication provider. Photos are stored in Supabase Storage. Data is stored on servers located in the United States.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Who Can See Your Information

  • Other users of OC Beach can see: your username, profile picture, posts, comments you make on public posts, your participation in group chats, and any beach conditions attached to your posts.
  • Other users CANNOT see: your email, your direct messages with other people, who you have blocked, or reports you have filed.
  • We may share information with law enforcement if required by valid legal process.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You can at any time:

  • Update your profile information (username, picture, home beach)
  • Delete individual posts, comments, or messages you created
  • Block other users to hide their content from you
  • Report content you believe violates community rules
  • Delete your account entirely from the Profile screen. Account deletion permanently removes your account, profile, direct messages, and personal posts. Comments and group chat messages remain but are anonymized.

California Residents (CCPA/CPRA): You have the right to know what personal information we collect, request deletion, and opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information. To exercise these rights, contact us using the email below.

6. Children's Privacy

OC Beach is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, your personal information is removed immediately, with the exceptions described in Section 5 (comments and group messages remain anonymized) and any legal retention obligations.

8. Security

We use Supabase's authentication and storage systems, which include industry-standard security measures (encryption in transit, row-level security, secure password hashing). No system is perfectly secure; you use the App at your own risk.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify users through the App or by email. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
