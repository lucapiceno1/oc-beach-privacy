Common questions

How do I delete my account?
Open the app → Profile tab → scroll to the bottom → tap "Delete account." This is permanent.

How do I block someone?
On a post, tap the "…" menu → tap "Block user." You can manage blocked users from Profile → Blocked users.

How do I report inappropriate content?
On a post, tap the "…" menu → tap "Report post" → choose a reason. We review reports manually.

I forgot my password.
On the sign-in screen, tap "Forgot password?" and follow the email instructions.

My posts don't show beach conditions.
Beach conditions only attach to posts where you select a beach. Community posts (no beach selected) don't include conditions.
